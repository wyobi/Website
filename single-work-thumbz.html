Description


Thumbzup is a London-based technology company with subsidiaries in Australia and South Africa, whose flagship product is the Payment Pebble, a secure mobile card reader that can plug into the audio jack of any Android or iOS device.


Wyobi is the bespoke developer of the iOS app which allows this product to work on Apple devices. This application is in use in two major banks: ABSA in South Africa, and ANZ in Australia and New Zealand.


Absa Payment Pebble App
The Payment Pebble™ is a mobile point-of-sale (mPOS) payment solution that securely processes credit and debit card transactions using the physical Payment Pebble device connected to your mobile phone via the 3.5mm audio jack. The Payment Pebble App from Absa along with the Payment Pebble device allows you to receive payments anywhere and at anytime at your convenience!
