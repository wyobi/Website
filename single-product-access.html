Description

Open Item Access control for large and small estates provides reporting, mobile applications for residents, keypads and a cloud connected hardware controller.

We provide integrated and turnkey solutions via our resellers:

  • Cloud-based always on Reporting
  • Keypads
  • Hardware controller with failover connections, Wifi, Kicker for Booms/Gates and more
  • SADL South African Drivers License Scanning
  • Motor License Disk Scanning
  • License Plate Recognition
  • Photo
  • Dynamic, customizable scanner applications (Ruggedized Devices, Android or other)
  • Mobile Apps for Apple iPhone as well as Android Phones
  • Pre-Bookings (Mass Import, SMS, App bookings)
  • Notifications and Alerts

We have around 30 estates using our mobile scanners, keypads, controllers, reporting or other technology. Contact us and we will direct you to the right provider for your access control needs.
