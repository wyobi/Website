What is Visitor Management?

Security is key

In today’s world security and access management is a necessity. But allowing adhoc/temporary access and logging and tracking unknown visitors is a pain. Estate- and corporate property management have tried to solve these issues by adding painful processes for visitors to be identified, pre-registered, or visit a welcome office before entering. This causes backlogs and increases workload and cost. These visitors are also rarely going to return so the effort is usually wasted. Enter OpenItem Access with its Visitor Mangement System. The product allows you to: Allow residents and employees (for corporate) to pre-book visitors on specific days via a branded mobile app or online. The visitor receives a pin via text message to ensure temporary access and link him to the pre-booking record. Identify the visitor via Drivers License Card, Identity Document or Passport (Hardware component, solution and country dependent) Record and Track who entered which gate/access point at what time and what day, as well as who they were invited by. Allows for non-invited visitors to pitch up, and allows guards to call the resident to be visited. The resident Pressing 9 during the call will allow access and link the identified visitor (ID Card/Doc, Drivers License, Passport or other) access. Modular, configureable and scalable to suit both small and larger estates, corporates or corporate estates.

Identity Verification

Data Reporting

The back-end allows you to track events and visitors and allows for piece of mind

Integration

API’s allow for integration with external Access Control systems to open gates or booms and allow for external reporting. A integration component also exists to allow for VMS to be integrated into your own already built mobile applications
